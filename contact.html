FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 3-6 months in advance for large events and weddings. For smaller events, 1-2 months is usually sufficient.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Chennai and surrounding areas in Tamil Nadu. For special events, we can arrange services across South India.

Do you provide customized packages?

Yes! We create customized packages tailored to your specific needs, budget, and preferences. Every event is unique.

What is your payment structure?

We typically require a 30% advance to confirm the booking, with the balance due before or on the event date. Payment plans available.
